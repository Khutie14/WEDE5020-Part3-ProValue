Business & Professional Value — Proposal

Project scope

This proposal outlines the work required to deliver a professional website and supporting digital presence for your business. Core areas covered:

  • Design and development of a responsive marketing website (Home, Services, About, Case Studies, Contact).
  • SEO basics: meta tags, sitemap, robots.txt and accessible structure.
  • Client-side interaction: FAQ accordion, gallery, dynamic posts and contact forms.
  • Testing and deployment guidance (GitHub and optional GitHub Pages).

Deliverables

  1. Fully responsive site with semantic HTML and CSS.
  2. Interactive JavaScript components (accordion, lightbox, dynamic posts loader).
  3. Contact & enquiry forms with validation (client-side demo + server integration guidance).
  4. README, changelog, robots.txt, sitemap.xml and evidence screenshots.

Timeline & milestones

  • Week 1 — Requirements & wireframes.
  • Week 2 — HTML/CSS implementation and responsive breakpoints.
  • Week 3 — JavaScript interactivity and forms.
  • Week 4 — Testing, accessibility checks and final handover.
